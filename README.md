# 🔥 Rifle-Torch — DIY Gas Grill Lighter

A grill lighter built into a toy rifle shell. A gas torch lives inside the plastic body:
piezo igniter throws the spark, the knob releases the gas and the flame comes out the barrel.

> **Heads up:** hobby project. You're working with flammable gas and open flame.
> Everything here is at your own risk
